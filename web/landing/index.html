FAQ

The short answers.

Is my data private?

Everything is stored locally on your device via Hive. The AI Coach sends only the snapshot it needs to generate a reflection, and the backend doesn't retain those requests. See Privacy Policy.

Does it work offline?

Yes — check-ins, routines, habits, and insights all work offline. The AI Coach needs a connection.

Can I export my data?

One tap in Settings → Data & Privacy gives you a JSON or CSV export of everything.

Does it really use AI?

Yes — Mood8 uses a large language model server-side to generate reflections, chat replies, and insight explanations from your own data.

What about Apple Watch?

A watchOS companion is in active development. The Wear OS build already ships.
